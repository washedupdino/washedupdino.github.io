process

Our development process

1 our five step process

Discover

We discuss the problem that you are looking to solve through software. Based on that, we integrate existing services that we know will solve that, or we start from scratch and build something from the ground up.

2 our five step process

Build

Our team begins with crafting a plan, including the technologies or services required. We then begin building the software while keeping the goal of solving your problem as the top priority. During this process we provide constant updates to you, the customer.

3 our five step process

Test

Once the initial build of the software is complete, we run tests to ensure it won't fail when your company is using it day to day.

4 our five step process

Training

After all tests have passed, we begin teaching you and your employees how to fully utilize this software so as to avoid confusion when it finally enters the workplace. Through individual training and documentation, we'll make sure everyone is up to speed.

5 our five step process

Deploy

When your team is comfortable navigating the software, we ship the final product! Once live, we are always available to troubleshoot and provide insight to those needing an extra hand.
