Our support & maintenance services

1 support & maintenance

Updates

Businesses and circumstances are always changing and your website should reflect that. Whether it be new services, limited time offers, or major changes in business operations (i.e. covid-19), we will append that information in a way that doesn't cause issues and fits your design.

2 support & maintenance

Monitoring

With common CMS websites (WordPress, Shopify, etc.) there are constant updates to themes, and plugins that you are using. We monitor these and actively update when they become available - ensuring that your website does not have any errors.

3 support & maintenance

SSL/TLS

Have you ever seen the broken lock saying "Not Secure" in the address bar of a website? That means that the website owners SSL/TLS certificate has expired. Hosting a website without that certificate can deter customers from dealing with your business and be detrimental to your SEO ranking. We provide a guaranteed renewal of the certificate to ensure this never happens on your website.
